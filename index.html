New Year Fest: Games Galore!

This event is proudly organized by Shri Shankarlal Sundarbai Shasun Jain College for Women, in collaboration with the Department HODs and Student Council


Step into the New Year with Enthusiasm and Fun!


Get ready to challenge your skills, unleash your creativity, and collaborate with your peers! Join us for an exciting lineup of games tailored for each department. This is your chance to showcase your talents, build team spirit, and kickstart the New Year with unforgettable memories. Don’t miss out—register now!


DEPARTMENTS-BASED GAMES

1.Technical: Debugging Challenge or Coding Quiz.
2.Creative: Logo Design or Story Building.
3.Management: Team Puzzle or Quick Task Game.
4.Finance: Budget Plan or Money Quiz.
5.General Fun: Treasure Hunt or Word Guess.

Instrctions for partipiants

1. Each team must consist of 3 members.
2. Participants should register before the deadline.
3. Teams must adhere to the allotted time for each game.
4. Respect the decisions of the organizers and judges.
5. Ensure fair play and maintain team spirit throughout the event.
